Cake hacks

Here are some boxed-mix hacks to make your cakes rich and flavorful.

  1. Start with the holy grail of cake hacks: Add an extra egg, swap oil for butter, and replace water with milk.
  2. Add hot water to chocolate boxed mix.
  3. Change the egg structure for a new texture.
  4. Use coffee instead of water.
  5. Add mayonnaise to your cake.
