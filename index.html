About Us

At The Cake Shop we are passionate about storytelling through the canvas of cake. In harmony with the rolling seasons, our cake and styling experts weave captivating narratives. Always evolving - and under the creative direction of Peggy Porschen herself - each season’s storyline flows seamlessly throughout our cakes & confectionery, Parlour exterior and interior installations and online shop.
