Welcome to the CakeMuseum family

If you’re looking for cakes, cupcakes, macarons, brownies & cookies that look like art & taste like dessert, you’re in the right place ☺ Whether you’re looking for a birthday cake, a baby shower cake, or some simpler treats for dessert, you can shop everything you need right here online. Browse over 100 cakes, choose your design, flavours, size & customisations, select a delivery or collection date & pay securely at checkout. Anything you order online will be ready within 48 hours! If you’re looking for something a little more last-minute or you feel like popping in to watch us create our edible art, visit our store in Cedar Square, Fourways. We stock a wide range of desserts including cakes-to-go in beautiful designs that are available 7 days a week, with no need to pre-order. If you have any questions, feel free to contact our team via WhatsApp or Email. Good things come to those who read the fine print – please see our Ts & Cs.
