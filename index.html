Cakes Co

Cakes Co is a family business founded in 1925 at Baker Street in London. Our cakes are baked using our iconic and finest ingredients. We undertake orders for parties, birthdays, weddings, etc. During the holiday season, we make special cakes using rich ingredients like cinnamon and cloves.
