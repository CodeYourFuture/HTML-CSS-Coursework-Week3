Welcome

The Cake Co. is a pastry shop and cafeteria that offers experiences to remember through everyday moments such as breakfast, a snack or brunch; where each elaborated product is designed to impress the visitor. At Capke we make absolutely everything we offer. We bake our bread, we think and make our pastry trying to innovate and tempt you through colorful and delicious cakes. We cook with generosity and love because it is the only way to create something transcendent that you can take with you, remember and wish for again. We offer an excellent blend of coffee designed to accompany our pastries or star in the break of the day. We make natural juices where we seek to combine new flavors that refresh and delight everyone. Afternoons with friends, dream celebrations, work breaks, sweet moments, friends' therapy, moments to enjoy... enjoy them at The Cake Co.
