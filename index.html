Welcome

Cake Co is London's only all natural bakery, delivering throughout the UK! Created by Harry Junior, playfulness is his passion; he loves to create exciting, experimental flavours and artisan designs, having fun in the process. Harry is a little health-conscious and like to make sure his bakes are irresistibly scrumptious without using too much fat and sugar - ‘just as much as needed to make something delicious’. Our premium bakes and gifts are lovingly handcrafted to taste delicious and look exquisite for you to enjoy whatever the occasion!
