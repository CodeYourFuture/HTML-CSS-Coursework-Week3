Welcome

When you order from Bakes by Joanna you know your cake made with the highest quality ingredients. We use only fresh free-range eggs, pure English butter, high-quality vanilla bean paste, and luxury unsweetened cocoa.
Just of the main streets of Wilmslow is Chapel lane. This is where our bakery is situated, set in a community of other family run businesses. Established since 1932 we certainly know a thing or to about baking but our hand have turned to a number of other areas including celebratory cakes.The celebratory cake department has certainly grown over the years and as award winning professionals we are constantly striving for that growing trend of “something different.” Due to this our clients travel from all over to be able to achieve their dream wedding cake.
