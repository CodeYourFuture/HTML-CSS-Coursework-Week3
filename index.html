Welcome

We strive to create the most innovative and tastiest products using the finest locally sourced ingredients. Whether it be a birthday party, new baby, hen party, anniversary or just because you've had a rubbish Monday, our baked goods not only look divine but taste delicious too and put a massive smile on anyone's face. We've been catering to Edinburgh folks cake requirements since 2011 and have been awarded the winner of the "Best Cake in Scotland" 2014/15, 2015/16, and 2017/18! We believe the proof of the pudding truly is in the eating!
