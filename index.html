Welcome

At Waynes' Cakes We make freshly baked celebration cakes for all occasions that can be delivered as a wonderful gift to a friend or relative or for events and parties. With generations of baking experience we know how to make brilliant cakes, delivered safely and ontime. Just take a look at our recent customers orders.
We offer a choice of vanilla, chocolate, lemon, coffee or red velvet sponge. You can chose from white chocolate Swiss meringue buttercream, Nutella buttercream, lemon curd, jam, coffee buttercream or indulgent cream cheese frosting for between the layers.
