Welcome

Here at Cake House we would like to think of each other as family, and our success is because we have held on to our family values. We have extended the family circle to include our customers. To us the customer is not somebody who simply buys a cake from one of our stores. When we make a cake for somebody’s special occasion, we know that we are being allowed into the person’s family via the cake. That is why each cake is baked and decorated as if it was meant for a member of one of our families.
